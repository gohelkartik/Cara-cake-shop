Who We Are?

Cara Cake Shop started with a single oven and a family recipe for the perfect velvet sponge. Today, we blend traditional French pastry techniques with modern flavor profiles. We believe that every cake tells a story—whether it's a grand wedding tier or a simple afternoon cupcake. Our bakers arrive before dawn to ensure that every treat is whisked, baked, and frosted to perfection using only organic, locally-sourced ingredients.
